Milo V2 is a larger, stiffer evolution of the OpenBuilds MiniMill lineage: moving-table **Milo** and stationary-X **Miley** share the same Y and Z core. **Mechanical assembly, firmware, and bring-up** are documented in **[MiloV2-Manual.pdf](https://github.com/MillenniumMachines/Milo-V2.0/blob/main/Manual/MiloV2-Manual.pdf)** in the Milo-V2.0 repository (canonical layout, figures, and callouts).

This site hosts **sourcing**, **printing**, and **FAQ** pages for Milo / Miley V2. Follow the PDF for the full build.

Electronics are **not** part of the Milo V2 repo. Use the [Universal Electronics Box](https://github.com/MillenniumMachines/Universal-Electronics-Box) project for control hardware and wiring.
