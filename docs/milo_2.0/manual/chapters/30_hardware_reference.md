# Hardware Reference

!!! note
    Text adapted from the Milo V2 assembly manual export. Diagrams from the official PDF will be added under `manual/img/` when assets are available.

BUTTON HEAD CAP SCREW (BHCS)

Low-profile metric fastener with a domed
head and hex socket drive.

DROP IN NUT

Post-install fastener that slides into a Tslot to provide a threaded anchor point.

SOCKET HEAD CAP SCREW (SHCS)

BELLEVILLE WASHER
Conical spring washer providing high load
in limited space.

High-strength metric fastener with a
cylindrical head and hex socket drive.

FLAT HEAD CAP SCREW (FHCS)

HEAT INSERT

Countersunk fastener with a flat top and
hex socket drive for a flush finish.


Threaded brass insert designed to be
heated and embedded into plastic parts.


GREASE NIPPLE

ROLLER BEARING

Fitting used to apply lubricant into
bearings or mechanical joints.

Rotating element that reduces friction
between moving parts using steel balls.

NEODYMIUM MAGNET

High-strength rare-earth magnet offering
powerful magnetic force in a compact size.

DOUBLE SPLIT SHAFT COLLAR
Clamp-style collar with two splits for
secure, even clamping around a shaft.

SPIDER COUPLER

THRUST WASHER

Flexible shaft coupling using an
elastomeric spider to absorb misalignment
and vibration.

Flat bearing surface that supports axial
loads and reduces friction.


FLAT WASHER

TIMING PULLEY
Toothed pulley designed to engage with a
timing belt for precise, slip-free power
transmission.

Flat disc used to distribute load and
prevent surface damage under a fastener.

#### Miley only

SET SCREW
Headless threaded fastener used to
secure one component to a shaft through
side pressure.

DOWEL PIN

Precision cylindrical pin used for accurate
alignment and positioning of components.

SHOULDER BOLT
Precision fastener with an unthreaded
shoulder for guiding, pivoting, or locating
parts.

LEADSCREW NUT

Threaded brass component that converts
rotary motion to linear motion on a
leadscrew.
