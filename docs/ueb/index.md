The **Universal Electronics Box (UEB)** is a modular DIN-rail electronics enclosure for Millennium CNC builds. It packages mains distribution, DC power, contactor control, and board mounts in a printable, extrusion-based frame with panel covers and integrated cable channels.

This site hosts the **bill of materials**, **printing settings and part list**, and a link to the **wiring reference PDF**. STLs, DXFs, and CAD live in the [Universal-Electronics-Box](https://github.com/MillenniumMachines/Universal-Electronics-Box) repository. Step-by-step wiring diagrams are in the PDF—not duplicated here.

!!! warning

    This enclosure carries **mains voltage**. Follow the wiring PDF, use an appropriate E-stop, and comply with your local electrical codes. If anything is unclear, ask on the Millennium Machines Discord before energizing the cabinet.

## What to expect

The wiring reference is titled **V2 / V1.6 wiring reference** and is intended alongside [Milo V2 / Miley V2](https://github.com/MillenniumMachines/Milo-V2.0). **Mechanical assembly, RRF install, and pre-flight checks** for the mill remain in **[MiloV2-Manual.pdf](https://github.com/MillenniumMachines/Milo-V2.0/blob/main/Manual/MiloV2-Manual.pdf)**. ## Repository layout

- **STLs** — panels, DIN mounts (Scylla, LRS-350), cable channel lids and trays, optional TPU feet
- **DXFs** — top/bottom and side metal or acrylic panels
- **CAD** — master assembly STEP for fit checks

Download printed parts from the repo after reviewing the [Printing Guide](./printing/print_guide.md).
